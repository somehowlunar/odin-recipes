Chicken Kiev

Lovely dish with a chicken breast stuffed with garlic and herb buter that bursts out when cut open

Enjoyed very much by many people - especially my friends

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Garlic and Herbs
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Butterfly chicken
  2. make garlic and herb butter
  3. put garlic and herb butter in chicken
  4. Roll up
  5. Panne and deep fry
